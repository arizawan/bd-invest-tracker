⚠️ Key Risk

BDT depreciates ~7-8% annually vs USD.

Any investment returning <8% in BDT loses real value in USD/THB terms.

Hedge: Keep 15-20% in USD-denominated instruments (USD Premium Bond).

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Open BRAC Bank NRB account (online)
  2. Buy Wage Earner Bond (12%)
  3. Buy USD Premium Bond (7% USD)
  4. Open BRAC EPL brokerage
  5. Invest in blue-chip stocks
